About Us

Connecting Polish graduate students and scholars at Harvard University. We organize cultural events, academic discussions, and social gatherings to promote Polish culture and create a supportive community for Polish students at Harvard.

Mission

We create a vibrant community for Polish graduate students and scholars at Harvard University, providing a platform for cultural exchange and networking.

Community

We welcome all members of the Harvard community interested in Polish culture, language, and affairs.

Culture

We celebrate Polish traditions, history, and contemporary affairs through various events and discussions.

Events & Activities

Cultural Events

  • Traditional celebrations
  • Cultural exchanges

Join us for cultural celebrations and events throughout the academic year. We host events featuring authentic Polish food, music, and customs to share our heritage with the Harvard community.
